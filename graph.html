          Sessions, Users, Organic Traffic
          (click the checkboxes to isolate data if needed)
        

        

          Sessions (Microsoft Clarity): A session is a group of user
          interactions with your website that take place within a given time
          frame. A session ends after 30 minutes of inactivity.
        

        

          Users (Google Analytics): A user is an individual who has
          visited your website at least once. Google Analytics tracks unique
          users across sessions.
        

        

          Organic Traffic (Ahrefs): Organic traffic refers to visitors
          who arrive at your website through unpaid search engine results.
        

      

      
      
      

        
Backlinks

        

          Backlinks (Ahrefs): Backlinks are incoming links from other
          websites to your site. They are a key factor in search engine ranking.
        

      

      
      
      

        
Domain Rating (DR)

        

          Domain Rating (DR) - Ahrefs: Domain Rating is a metric that
          measures the strength of a website's backlink profile on a scale from
          0 to 100.
        

      

      
      
      

        
Bounce Rate

        

          Bounce Rate (Google Analytics): Bounce rate is the percentage
          of visitors who navigate away from your site after viewing only one
          page.
        

      

      
      
      

        
Click-Through Rate

        

          Click-Through Rate (Google Search Console): The click-through
          rate (CTR) is the percentage of users who clicked on your website's
          link after seeing it in search engine results.
        

      

      
      
      

        
Average Position

        

          Average Position (Google Search Console): Average position is
          the average ranking of your website's pages in search engine results
          for a specific query
        

      

      
      
      

        
Time On Page

        

          Time on Page (Microsoft Clarity): Time on page refers to the
          average duration a visitor spends on a specific page on your website.
        

      

      
      
      

        
Average Engagement Time

        

          Average Engagement Time: The average amount of time users spend
          actively interacting with your website or app. It shows how long, on
          average, people are engaged with your content, excluding times when
          they are inactive.
